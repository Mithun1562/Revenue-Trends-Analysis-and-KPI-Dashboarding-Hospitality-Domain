# Analyzing-and-dashboarding-KPI-s-and-key-insights-on-Revenue-trends-in-Hospitality-domain



I worked on a hospitality analytics project where I analyzed hotel performance using KPIs like occupancy, ADR, RevPAR, and realization rate.

I found that while the ADR was relatively high (~₹11.7K), occupancy was only 53%, indicating pricing inefficiencies.

I also identified a high cancellation rate of around 25%, which significantly impacted revenue realization (~69%).

Additionally, weekend occupancy was higher than weekdays, showing demand imbalance.

I built Power BI dashboards to visualize these insights and suggest improvements in pricing, demand planning, and booking strategies




Analyzed hospitality data across multiple cities to evaluate revenue (₹55.77M), occupancy (53.4%), and pricing performance

Built Power BI dashboards tracking KPIs such as RevPAR (₹6.25K), ADR (₹11.7K), and Realisation (69.34%)

Identified high cancellation rate (~25%), highlighting revenue leakage and customer behavior issues

Discovered demand imbalance with higher weekend occupancy (57.6%) vs weekday (51.7%), indicating underutilized capacity

Analyzed booking platform performance, uncovering variations in realisation rates (65–70%)

Generated insights to optimize pricing strategy, reduce cancellations, and improve occupancy rates
